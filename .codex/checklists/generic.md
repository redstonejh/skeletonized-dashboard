# Generic Risk Checklist

Task type: `generic`

- Public tests and deterministic test commands pass. Evidence: `artifacts/test-result.json`
- Handoffs and plan-gate evidence are complete before final acceptance. Evidence: `artifacts/acceptance-result.json`
- Hidden behavior, golden outputs, and edge-case assumptions are considered. Evidence: `advisory critic-only`
- Claims in the final answer are supported by artifacts or deterministic output. Evidence: `advisory critic-only`
